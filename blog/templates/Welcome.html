Attendance

At times we miss our classes maybe because we were ill, had some other important task at hand or maybe just too lazy to go to class but we have to ensure that our attendance is above certain percentage. So at click of button you can add your day to day attendance and will warn you when the need arrives of how many classes you need to attend.

Calendar

Which Fest is around the corner ? When are the Mid-Semester Examination starting ? What is the duration of Mid-Semester Break ? No need to bother your friends anymore with these questions we have it all sorted for you. And not just that you can go on and create events of your own too. We will keep you updated with events happening in your institute.

Academic Stuff

Looking around for basic stuffs like syllabus of a course or maybe a place of keep all your notes so that you can access them remotely can be difficult at times. Also we need our identity with us to show it for entry into a pro-nite or for attendance purposes. So we bring these three pieces together as a single set of academic tools.
